Designing Your Project

My approach to design is collaborative and we will work together to create a successful web application/website to help you promote your work, sell your products, or advertise your services. Your customized layout will help elevate your business and enhance the success you have already worked hard to achieve!

Consultation
We will discuss your goals, vision, timeframe, and budget as well as answer any questions you may have.

Proposal
Based on our consultation, I will draft a contract and we can review it together to ensure we are on the same page and that you will get the perfect web application or website for your needs.

Development
I carefully construct your project within our agreed upon timeframe while providing you with weekly updates regarding my progress.

Delivery
I transfer the project and all supporting files to you while also answering any questions you may have.

What You Can Expect

About Me

I am Jaclyn Brothers and I love supporting people's businesses and professional careers by helping them realize their vision for their internet presence. I use my background as a former speech therapist to effectively communicate and coordinate with you to ensure we are on the same page and you get the final product that completely meets your needs.

At the beginning of the COVID-19 pandemic, many of my students had difficulties using and participating with virtually provided materials. I was able to develop projects that increased student accessibility, engagement, learning, and mastery of skills. This sparked my fascination for developing web applications individually and within teams that further increase user accessibility and satisfaction. I have also built modern and responsive websites using best practices for clients. I enjoy collaborating and look forward to meeting you! When I am not programming, I enjoy being out in nature, listening to music, and watching NBA games.
